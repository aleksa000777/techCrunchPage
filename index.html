Movie Chain Game

The Chain Game is a fun game that tests your movie knowledge! The game begins by giving you the name of an actor. Your job is to name a movie that this actor appears in. If you name a movie that the actor appears in, the next person in the game then has to name another actor in this movie.

The game continues to go back and forth between different Movies and Actors in the Movies until someone gives an incorrect answer. If an incorrect answer is given, Alexa will scold the player and the game comes to an end. How many can you get in a row???
